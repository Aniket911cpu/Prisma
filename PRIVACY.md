# Privacy Policy for Prisma

**Last Updated: December 28, 2025**

Your privacy is important to us. It is Prisma's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website and extension.

## 1. Information We Collect

Prisma is designed to be privacy-focused. 

- **We do not collect personal information.** 
- **We do not track your browsing history.**
- **We do not send data to external servers.**

All data (color history, settings) is stored locally on your device using the Chrome Storage API (`chrome.storage.local`).

## 2. Permissions

Prisma requires specific permissions to function:

- **activeTab / Scripting**: Used strictly to enable the EyeDropper tool on the current page. We do not read page content other than the pixel you click.
- **Storage**: Used to save your preferences and color history locally.

## 3. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

## 4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@prisma-extension.com.
